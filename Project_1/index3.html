ABOUT US

They are absolutely sure: coffee is not just a tonic. He is filled with life, which can be made more rich and interesting, cheered up when necessary, or bring a few minutes of relaxation and enjoyment. And if we add some more bright creative elements, then we get "Coffee ... Art ... Life ...". This is how the slogan of our cafe. By the way, the name of the art-cafe has a deeper meaning than it seems at first glance. White is a mixture of all colors, and a suitcase is an object in which everyone can put what he needs. So, coming to the coffee house, the guest can take what he wants at the moment: a hot espresso with a nutty shade, halvic cocoa of vanilla color or a fresh croissant.

In our cafe very carefully monitor the quality of coffee. The coffee card is quite wide. It features all the popular coffee drinks that are prepared using only natural ingredients. For syrup lovers, there are about 15 kinds of different tastes from Monin, which are distinguished by their maximum naturalness and high quality. There is also an extensive tea card (more than 10 varieties) and several types of cocoa. In addition, every month a new author's drink will appear on the menu, created on the basis of the history and culture of various countries. Guests can not only try it, but also learn about it interesting facts.

VIEW MENU Design. The design used light colors, wood and unobtrusive color details. The interior is distinguished by a lot of things handmade — lamps, decor elements. All wooden elements, including furniture, were made to order.
